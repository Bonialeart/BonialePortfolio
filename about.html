About Me

Alejandro Bonilla

Hello! I'm Alejandro Bonilla, a Venezuelan illustrator. I'm a passionate artist who loves bringing characters and worlds to life. My journey began as a hobby, but over time, my passion turned into a career.

I specialize in concept art, illustration, and character design, always aiming to create imaginative and detailed worlds.

When I'm not working on my art, I enjoy learning new techniques and sharing my knowledge with others.
